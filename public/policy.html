PRIVACY POLICY

LAST UPDATED: OCTOBER 26, 2023

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CNS Studios ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our website and services (collectively, "Services").

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, and information we get from your use of our Services, such as your IP address.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our Services, to develop new ones, and to protect us and our users.

4. Information We Share

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations and individuals outside of CNS Studios unless one of the following circumstances applies: with your consent, for external processing, or for legal reasons.

5. GDPR Rights for European Users

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. We aim to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data.

6. Data Security

We work hard to protect our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@cns.studio
